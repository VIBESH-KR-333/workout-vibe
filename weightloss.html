Overview

This plan focuses on calorie burn, metabolic conditioning, and increasing overall fitness levels. Ideal for people aiming to lose weight safely and effectively.

Warm-up & Cooldown

  • Warm-up: 5–10 min light cardio (jumping jacks, brisk walking)
  • Cooldown: Stretch legs, back, and shoulders for 5–10 min

Weekly Schedule

Day Exercise Sets Reps / Time

Exercise Descriptions

  • Burpees: Jump, squat, and push-up in one fluid movement for max calorie burn.
  • Russian Twists: Keep core tight, rotate slowly side to side.
  • Circuit Training: Perform exercises back-to-back with minimal rest.

FAQs & Notes

  • Hydrate well before and after workouts.
  • Track calories and progress weekly.
  • Modify intensity based on fitness level.
