Overview

This plan is designed to help beginners build a solid fitness foundation, learn proper form, and establish a consistent workout routine. Ideal for those new to strength training or returning after a long break.

Warm-up & Cooldown

  • Warm-up: 5–10 minutes light cardio (jumping jacks, brisk walking, or cycling)
  • Cooldown: 5–10 minutes stretching focusing on legs, chest, and back

Weekly Schedule

Exercise Descriptions

  • Bodyweight Squats: Keep your back straight, go as low as comfortable, and push through heels.
  • Push-ups: Hands shoulder-width apart, lower chest toward the floor, keep core tight.
  • Plank: Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels, engage core.

FAQs & Notes

  • Rest 30–60 seconds between sets.
  • Listen to your body; avoid pain and overtraining.
  • Track your progress weekly for motivation.
