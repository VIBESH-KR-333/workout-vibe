Overview

This high-intensity plan is for experienced individuals who want to maximize strength, power, and endurance using advanced techniques and training methods.

Warm-up & Cooldown

  • Warm-up: 10 minutes dynamic stretches and light cardio
  • Cooldown: 10 minutes stretching with foam rolling optional

Weekly Schedule

Day Exercise Sets Reps / Time
Day 1Deadlifts48
Day 2Pull-ups48
Day 3Weighted Squats48
Day 4Superset Core Work (Planks & Hanging Leg Raises)3-
Day 5HIIT Cardio-20–30 min

Exercise Descriptions

  • Deadlifts: Maintain flat back, lift with legs and hips, not lower back.
  • Pull-ups: Full range of motion, shoulders engaged, avoid swinging.
  • HIIT Cardio: Alternate high-intensity and low-intensity intervals.

FAQs & Notes

  • Rest 90–120 seconds between heavy sets.
  • Focus on form over weight to avoid injury.
  • Include adequate protein intake to support recovery.
