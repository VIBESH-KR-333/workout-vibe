Overview

This advanced 7-day plan is designed for experienced individuals to maximize strength, endurance, and conditioning using high-intensity and compound exercises.

Warm-up & Cooldown

  • Warm-up: 10 minutes dynamic stretches + light cardio
  • Cooldown: 10 minutes stretching + foam rolling

Weekly Schedule

Exercise Descriptions

  • Bench Press: Lie on bench, press barbell up, elbows slightly bent at top.
  • Deadlifts: Maintain flat back, lift using legs and hips, not lower back.
  • Squats: Keep back straight, push through heels, go as low as comfortable.
  • Plank: Keep body in a straight line from head to heels, core engaged.
  • Burpees: Jump, squat, and push-up in one fluid movement.
  • Russian Twists: Core tight, rotate slowly side to side.

FAQs & Notes

  • Rest 90–120 seconds between heavy sets.
  • Focus on form over weight to prevent injuries.
  • Ensure adequate hydration and nutrition.
  • Track progress weekly for strength and endurance improvements.
