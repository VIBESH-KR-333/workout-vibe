Beginner Plan

Focus: Foundation, correct form, consistency Frequency: 3–4 days/week Workout: Warm-up (5–10 min light cardio) Bodyweight squats – 3×10 Push-ups (kneeling if needed) – 3×8 Plank – 3×20 sec Light cardio/cooldown (5–10 min)

Intermediate Plan

Focus: Strength + Endurance Frequency: 4–5 days/week Workout: Warm-up (dynamic stretches + light cardio) Squats (with weight) – 4×10 Push-ups or Bench Press – 4×10 Dumbbell Rows – 4×12 Plank (longer hold) – 3×30 sec 15–20 min cardio (moderate)

Advanced Plan

Focus: High-intensity training & advanced techniques Frequency: 5–6 days/week Workout: Warm-up Deadlifts – 4×8 Pull-ups – 4×8 Weighted Squats – 4×8 Superset core work (plank variations, hanging leg raises)

20–30 min HIIT cardio 2–3× per week

Weight Loss Plan

Focus: Calorie burn & metabolic conditioning Frequency: 5 days/week Workout: Warm-up Circuit of: Jumping Jacks, Squats, Push-ups, Burpees (30 sec each, 3–5 rounds) Strength: Light weights, high reps (12–15) Cardio: 30–40 min brisk walking/jogging Core finisher: Plank & Russian twists.

Muscle Gain Plan

Focus: Hypertrophy + Progressive Overload Frequency: 4–6 days/week Workout Split Example: Day 1: Chest & Triceps Day 2: Back & Biceps Day 3: Legs Day 4: Shoulders & Core Rest or repeat Rep range: 8–12 reps, 3–5 sets, progressive overload
