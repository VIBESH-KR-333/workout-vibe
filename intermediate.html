Overview

This plan is for those who want to increase strength and endurance. It is ideal for individuals who have completed a beginner program and want to progress to higher intensity workouts.

Warm-up & Cooldown

  • Warm-up: Dynamic stretches + 5–10 min light cardio
  • Cooldown: Stretch all major muscle groups for 5–10 min

Exercise Descriptions

  • Squats (with weight): Maintain straight back and push through heels; use dumbbells or barbell.
  • Dumbbell Rows: Keep back flat, pull dumbbell toward waist, squeeze shoulder blades.
  • Plank: Straight line from head to heels, core engaged.

FAQs & Notes

  • Rest 60–90 seconds between sets.
  • Progress weight gradually, avoid overloading too fast.
  • Track your performance to monitor improvements.
